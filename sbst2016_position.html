What is Digital Piracy?

Digital piracy is a form of online piracy and includes the unauthorized online distribution of electronic copies of copyrighted material such as software, movies, and music.

Affected Demographic

80% of the students do not see any problem with downloading music from the Internet without paying for it

Solutions

Limit the restrcitions on piracy

Let artists independently go after people who pirate content instead of making it a federal crime

Good or Bad?

Piracy prohibits artists and software developers from getting the money and recognition they deserve

The distribution of pirated content helps with exposure and makes people's work available to a larger audience
